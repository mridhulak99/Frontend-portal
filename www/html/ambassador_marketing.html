About the program


Clubs4All campus ambassador program is a platform for students passionate about networking and marketing to help us reach the target audience in their campus.

Clubs4All campus ambassadors will implement the action plans for promoting us and onboarding users on our website on their campus





What's in it for you?

A monthly stipend of Rs.X

Opportunity to get listed as a contributor to our club on our website, and recognition on our social media handle.

Networking opportunity with other campus ambassadors


What are your responsibilities?


Collect information on various clubs and student bodies at your campus.

Connect with various club leads/secretaries to communicate the benefits of listing their clubs with us.

Send e-mails, posters and any other form of communication from us to entire batch

Provide us feedback from users to improve the user experience.
