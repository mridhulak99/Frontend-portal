Vogue - Fashion Event

Description

Every designer fantasizes and every fashionista dreams of showcasing these fantasies through their talent. Vogue provides an opportunity to showcase on the very theme of fantasy. Come, transform your fantasies into reality. Maybe your reality into a fantasy.


Rules

Team Size

Designers:1-4

Models: 8-14

Choreographer: 1

Makeup Artists: 1-4 (excluding the models)

Theme Description

From Reverie to Reality “Weaving fantasies”

Additional Element - At least 2 outfits MUST feature BODITECTURE (Convertible fashion construction)

Judging Criteria

Designers: Theme Originality, Depiction of Theme, Designing, Use of Props and Accessories, Use of fabrics

Models: Ramp walk, Stage Presence, Poise, Overall Appearance

Team: Choreography, Music and Narration, Co-ordination, Relevance to the theme, Overall Performance
